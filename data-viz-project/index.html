How spoiled are dogs in the US?

Paige Trendell

April 18, 2026

Dog ownership in the United States has increased substantially over the past three decades, according to the American Veterinary Medical Association. In present day, it seems like Americans are viewing their dogs as "man's best friend" quite literally. Data from a Forbes Advisor study conducted in 2022 shows that almost half of Californians have pushed their dog(s) in a stroller. In some states, over 10 percent of people have factored in their dog(s) when making career choices.

The top 10 states of each data set vary. Only four states (Delaware, New York, Texas and Colorado) are in both charts.
